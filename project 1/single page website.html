VAS HOTELS VISAKHAPATNAM

Hotel that makes every moment matter

Spoil yourself in 5-star seafront luxury at VAS HOTELS VISAKHAPATNAM.Spare contemporary design makes your room a calm haven, and picture windows offer you dazzling Bay of Bengal views. Rise and shine with a dip in the infinity pool and breakfast with an ocean backdrop at the lavish Indian and Western buffet at Hotel. The hotel shares a close proximity with the city railway station, Rushikonda Beach, Vizag Port and major hospitals. The popular Submarine Museum is just a walk away. The hotel's city centre location makes it approachable from industrial and business hubs. Vas Hotel Vizag forms part of a complex consisting of an INOX multiplex, night club and mall. Vas Hotel Visakhapatnam Varun Beach is among the only hotels in Visakhapatnam near a beach. The hotel offers calming views of the sea from every nook and corner.
